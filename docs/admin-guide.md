## Managing Inventory

### Adding a Vehicle
1. Click **ADD NEW VEHICLE** on the dashboard.
2. Fill in the required details:
   - **VIN:** Must be exactly 17 characters.
   - **Title Status:** Standard for USA market (Clean, Rebuilt, Salvage).
   - **Main Image:** Enter the filename of the image stored in the frontend assets.
3. Click **SAVE VEHICLE**.

### Editing and Status Updates
- Use the **EDIT** button to correct details.
- Toggle the **STATUS** button (AVAILABLE / SOLD) directly from the list to quickly mark vehicles as sold. This removes them from active public results if filtering is applied.

## Managing Leads
1. Navigate to the **LEADS** tab.
2. Review incoming inquiries from potential buyers.
3. Update the status:
   - **NEW:** Just received.
   - **CONTACTED:** You have reached out to the buyer.
   - **CLOSED:** Deal finished or inquiry resolved.

## Security Note
Always click **LOGOUT** when finished to clear your session token from the browser.
